Dashboards

TenderRadar: procurement visibility with a bid / no-bid brain.

This page is now the live home of TenderRadar. The goal is not to dump every tender into a noisy feed. The goal is to help Indian SMEs see which bids fit, why they fit, and where qualification gaps will block them.

Fit before volume

Procurement products become useful when they rank opportunities, not when they list everything.

Qualification visible

SMEs need turnover, credential, and urgency signals exposed fast before a team wastes bid effort.

Source-aware

Every opportunity should feel traceable back to the source portal, not hallucinated out of a black box.

TenderRadar deep dive.

The first dashboard page is now a real product surface. This V1 focuses on profile-aware procurement triage for Indian SMEs, with room to expand into live portal ingestion and alerting.

Design Principles

What good dashboard work should feel like.

  • Fast scanning before deep reading.
  • Signal above decoration.
  • Dense information without visual panic.
  • Clear paths from summary to action.
